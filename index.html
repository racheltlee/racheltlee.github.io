Why the Peace Corps?

As an avid follower of the daily news, I've closely followed human rights crises, environmental problems, and political tensions across the world. Impoverished and marginalized communities are hurt in unimaginable ways: indigenous populations are hurt by violent deforestation efforts, contract abuse is rampant the Fast Fashion industry, and young women are sold into human trafficking rings by their families. During my service, I want to make a difference, no matter how small, and empower young people. Education is power and every bit of knowledge can help end the global poverty cycle!
